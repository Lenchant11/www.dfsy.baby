Our Story

Founded in 2022 ‚Ä¢ Global Team

DfsyTask was founded on a simple premise: work should be flexible, fair, and accessible to everyone. We noticed a gap in the market for short, verified tasks that respected workers' time while providing businesses with reliable results.

Starting with a small team of engineers and operations experts, we built a platform that emphasizes transparency in payments, clear task instructions, and robust quality controls. Today, we serve thousands of workers and hundreds of businesses worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is DfsyTask based?
We are a fully remote company with team members across the globe. Our legal entity is registered in Singapore, but we operate internationally to serve workers and businesses worldwide.
How do you ensure fair pay for workers?
We provide suggested payout ranges for different task types and durations, based on market research. Employers can choose to pay above these suggestions. We also have minimum payout thresholds to prevent exploitation.
What types of businesses use DfsyTask?
Our clients range from startups needing data labeling for machine learning models, to mid-sized companies conducting user research, to large enterprises running large-scale surveys or content moderation.
How can I join the DfsyTask team?
Check our careers page for open positions. We're always looking for talented people who share our mission of creating fair, flexible work opportunities.
